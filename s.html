Rotation 1: Stamp



Project Description:


For my first project I chose to design a stamp. I watched several videos to understand how to use Fusion 360. The process was not too diffucult to learn because I used SolidWorks last year. For the stamp, I did not use the video's design, I wanted to make mine different. I started off by inserting a cylindar to create a round base. Then I made a taller and thinner cylindar ontop. As a handle I used a sphere. It is a relatively simple design combining shapes. To personalize my stamp I skeched my initials. Sketching my initias was the hardest part of the process. Making the letters look correct proved to be a challenge, but I got to experiment with different sketch tools; eclipse, spline, and trim. The wax was an experiment. My seal is not as clear as I would like it to be. It was diffucult finding the perfect time to press the stamp into the wax.
